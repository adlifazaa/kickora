FULL DESCRIPTION (max 4000 characters)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Kickora is a premium, lightweight football live scores app built for fans who want clarity, speed, and a polished experience — in English or Arabic.

Follow live and upcoming matches, explore competitions, dive into match details, and keep your favorite teams and leagues close. Kickora is designed to feel fast and focused, without clutter.

WHAT YOU CAN DO

• Home — Live matches, today’s fixtures, and featured competitions at a glance
• Matches — Live, upcoming, and finished tabs with date browsing
• Match details — Scores, status, events, statistics, lineups, and formations
• Competitions — Browse leagues and tournaments; open competition details
• Standings — League tables where data is available
• Favorites — Save teams and competitions for quick access
• Settings — Language (English / Arabic), light & dark theme, legal & support
• Notifications-ready — Architecture prepared for match and favorite alerts

WHY KICKORA

• Premium, clean interface — easy to read at a glance
• Full Arabic (RTL) and English (LTR) support
• Dark mode and light mode
• Thoughtful layout for match cards, stats, and competition browsing
• Built for real football data when connected to a data provider
