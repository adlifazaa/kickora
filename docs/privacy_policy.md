# Kickora Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** May 2026  
**Contact:** support@kickora.app (placeholder)

## Overview

Kickora (`com.kickora.worldcup`) is a football scores and statistics app. This policy describes what data we process and why.

## Data we collect

Kickora does not require sign-in or an account. We do not collect your name, email, or phone number.

- **App usage (Firebase Analytics):** when Firebase is connected, Google Firebase Analytics receives screen views and feature events (e.g. match or competition opened, favorites added, app open). Search activity is logged as query length only — not search text. We do not log API keys or FCM tokens in analytics.
- **Crash reports (Firebase Crashlytics):** when Firebase is connected, crash logs, stack traces, and device/OS metadata are sent to Google to diagnose and fix stability issues.
- **Push notifications (Firebase Cloud Messaging):** the FCM SDK is integrated. Notifications are off by default. If you enable match alerts in Settings, your device is registered with Firebase for the topics you choose (e.g. favorite teams). You can disable notifications at any time in the app or system settings.
- **Advertising (Google Mobile Ads / AdMob):** the AdMob SDK is included but real ads are not shown in the current release. When ads are enabled in a future build, AdMob may collect advertising identifiers and process data per Google's AdMob policies. Premium subscribers do not see ads.
- **In-app purchases (Google Play):** purchase and subscription state is processed by Google Play; we store premium entitlement locally on your device only.
- **Local preferences:** language, theme, favorites, and notification preferences are stored on your device (SharedPreferences) and are not transmitted to Kickora servers.
- **Football data:** by default the app displays mock/demo data. When live data is configured, match/team/competition requests may be sent to third-party football data providers; no personal information about you is included in those requests.

## Data we do not sell

We do not sell your personal information.

## Your choices

- Turn off notifications in app Settings.
- Clear local data by uninstalling the app or clearing app data in system settings.
- Cancel Premium via Google Play subscription management.
- Where Google/Firebase settings allow, you may limit certain analytics or ad personalisation through your device or Google account settings.

## Children

Kickora is not directed at children under 13.

We may update this policy; continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.
