# Kickora Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** May 2026  
**Contact:** support@kickora.app (placeholder)

## Overview

Kickora (`com.kickora.live`) is a football scores and statistics app. This policy describes what data we process and why.

## Data we collect

- **App usage (Firebase Analytics):** screen views and feature events (e.g. match opened, favorites). We do not log search query text, API keys, or FCM tokens in analytics.
- **Crash reports (Firebase Crashlytics):** stack traces and device metadata to fix crashes.
- **Push notifications (Firebase Cloud Messaging):** device registration for topics you subscribe to when you enable notifications in Settings. You can disable notifications at any time.
- **Advertising (Google AdMob):** when ads are enabled, AdMob may collect advertising identifiers per Google’s policies. Premium subscribers do not see ads.
- **In-app purchases (Google Play):** purchase state is processed by Google; we store premium entitlement locally on your device.
- **Football data:** match, team, and competition information is fetched from configured football data providers (direct API in development, backend proxy in production). We do not sell personal data.

## Data we do not sell

We do not sell your personal information.

## Your choices

- Turn off notifications in app Settings.
- Use mock/offline mode without live API credentials.
- Cancel Premium via Google Play subscription management.

## Children

Kickora is not directed at children under 13.

## Changes

We may update this policy; continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.
